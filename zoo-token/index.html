How to buy ZOOT on PancakeSwap

Download Metamask

For Desktop: Turn on your Google Chrome or Brave then visit metamask.io. Download the MetaMask extension and set up a wallet. On mobile: Get MetaMask’s app for IOS or Android.

Buy BNB

Use an exchange to buy BNB. Some exchanges are Binance, Binance US and Coinbase.

Transfer BNB to Metamask

Transfer BNB to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet BNB BEP-20 addresses start with 0x, you can see the address on metamask or your BEP-20 crypto wallet by clicking on Buy or Receive on Metamask/Crypto Wallet.

When on Zootoken.io

Click "Buy on PancakeSwap" button. This will take you to PancakeSwap website. You will receive a pop up asking to confirm if the address belongs to $ZOOT.

When on PancakeSwap

Set your slippage to 3-4% and enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap for $ZOOT.

Swap BNB for ZOOT

Confirm with Metamask.

View your ZOOT in Metamask

Why Ethereum Blockchain?

Despite many networks' recent progress, the Ethereum blockchain remains a pioneer and is continually expanding. Many users and developers are pleased with recent Etherscan upgrades. More significantly, many scam and rug-pull projects continue to be developed. The Ethereum blockchain's high gas charge will partially filter out low-quality projects. As a result, we chose Ethereum Blockchain as the ZooToken ecosystem's stable base. At the same time, finding reliable strategic partners is becoming simpler for us.

How will ZooPrediction work?

We will select at least two projects to participate in each ZooPrediction race (usually 5-10 projects as the ecosystem has grown). Zoot investors will have a few minutes to bet on the coin with the fastest growth rate in a specified time period. The winners will share (proportionally) 95 percent of the losers wagers. As a subscription charge, the remaining 5% will be allocated to the liquidity pool.

What is the purpose of fundraising?

In addition to raising funds to develop the project, ZooToken Finance will establish a fund to assist international zoos that are experiencing financial hardship as a result of the COVID pandemic. This fund is called ZooRelief Fund.
The ZooRelief Fund will be distributed to zoo management structures in countries that are facing financial hardship as a result of COVID 19. This money would be used to keep zoos solvent during the pandemic.
Fifty percent (50%) of the proceeds from the ZooPrediction, ZooIndex, ZooPad, and ZooNFT will be contributed to animal rescue. To efficiently disperse the funds, we will focus on areas where relief is required most
